# Chocolate Factory Sales Project - PowerBI

![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/Image%201.JPG)

## Introduction

This project transforms a chocolate factory sales dataset into a fully interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide clarity on revenue performance, sales team effectiveness, and future growth potential.

The goal was not just to visualize data, but to create a structured decision-support system that answers three core business questions:

- How are we performing overall?

- Who is driving our sales success?

- Where are we headed next?

To elevate the experience beyond a traditional BI report, I first designed the dashboard layout in PowerPoint, crafting a clean, modern interface and then imported it into Power BI. All data cleaning, modeling, KPI creation, and visualization were executed within Power BI.

The result is a polished, insight-driven dashboard that blends strong analytics with thoughtful design.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is intentionally structured as a guided business journey, consisting of three dedicated and interconnected pages. Each page answers a different layer of decision-making needs.

### Page 1: Sales Summary – Portfolio-Level Performance
![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/Image%201.JPG)

This page provides a high-level overview of overall factory performance.

It focuses on answering:

- What is the total revenue generated?

- Which region generates the most revenue?

- Which products contribute the most to revenue?

- Are we experiencing growth or decline?

Key KPIs are positioned prominently for immediate clarity, while supporting visuals show revenue distribution, time trends, and product breakdown.

This page is designed for executives who need insight within seconds — a clear snapshot of sales health.


### Page 2: Sales Person Performance – Driving Accountability
![Dashboard Page 2](/Images/Image%202.JPG)

Sales growth depends on individual contribution.

The Sales Person Performance page evaluates team effectiveness through:

- Revenue generated per sales representative

- Performance rankings

- Comparative analysis

- Contribution percentages

This page enables stakeholders to identify:

- Top performers

- Underperforming areas

- Sales imbalances

- Opportunities for coaching and strategy adjustment

It transforms raw sales figures into actionable performance intelligence.


### Page 3: Future Forecast – Planning with Confidence
![Dashboard Page 3](/Images/Image%203.JPG)

Understanding the past is valuable. Predicting the future is powerful.

The Future Forecast page leverages historical data trends to visualize projected sales performance. Instead of relying on assumptions, this page provides data-driven insights into:

- Anticipated revenue trends

- Growth trajectories

- Seasonal fluctuations

- Potential future sales performance

This forward-looking approach supports proactive strategy rather than reactive decision-making.
